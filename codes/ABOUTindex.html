How it all started

Everything started with one person's simple decision to start working out and lift weights.

A decision that quickly developed into a continuous motivation to learn fitness literacy in detail

in order to reach better results in training.

A motivation that developed into an obsession of limitlessly learning more and more about how to gain weight.

An obsession leading to the vision of preventing people to make the same mistakes he learned from on his route.

A vision that was expanded to the idea of cleanmuscle as an app offering the services that would have prevented the obstacles that had been faced throughout the process.

If he had had cleanmuscle as you have today, his progress would have vastly increased.
